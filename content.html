Berkeley Build Guild

What?

A gathering of teen makers building cool hardware projects. Attendees will be put into teams of two. Each team will get a set of electronics supplies including a programmable microcontroller and components.

Who?

The event is open to all makers aged 13-18.

When?

Saturday, April 18,

10:15 am to 1:00 pm

Where?

Berkeley Public Library West Branch, Community Meeting Room

Community Meeting Room, 1125 University Ave. (at San Pablo)

Berkeley, CA 94702

Teams and Provided Materials

Each team is made up of two people. You may come with a friend or find a teammate at the event. Teams are welcome to work together and share supplies.

FAQs

What should I bring?

At least one person from each team should bring:

  • Laptop
  • Laptop charger

Recommended for each attendee to bring:

  • USB-A to USB-C cable
  • Any additional electronics components that you want to use for your project
  • Snacks in case you get hungry

Will there be adult supervision?

There will be an adult present in the room and parents are welcome to sit in the main part of the library located just outside the room.
